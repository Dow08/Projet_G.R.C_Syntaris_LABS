### Documented Assumptions
- Infrastructure: Hybrid Cloud (AWS/Azure for transaction engine, on-prem for corporate).
- Data Scope: Personal financial data (PCI-DSS), biometric data (GDPR Art. 9, KYC), corporate identity databases.
- Top 3 Business Risks:
  1. Breach of Biometric & Identity Data (KYC) -> Severe GDPR and regulatory penalties.
  2. Payment Service Unavailability (DDoS, Cloud outage) -> SLA breaches and financial losses.
  3. Software Supply Chain Compromise (API tampering) -> Infiltration of malicious payment interceptors.
